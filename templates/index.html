About

Hi there! My name is Vinicius Enari and I am a recent graduate of Minerva University, where I earned my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. In addition to my passion for programming, I also enjoy playing video games, listening to music and playing the saxophone and electric guitar.

As a programmer, I have experience building and utilizing APIs, and building web applications with Flask, Django, and React. I have strong skills in Python, as well as a solid understanding of JavaScript, C#, PHP, and Rust. I am also open to learning any new language or technology if it is needed for a project or task.

I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow as a professional. I am excited to apply my skills and experience to make a meaningful contribution to any team or project I am a part of. Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio and learn more about me.
